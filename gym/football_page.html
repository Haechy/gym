Bijlmer Sportpark

Sportpark Bijlmer, nestled within the scenic Mandela Park, is a vibrant hub for football enthusiasts. This field encapsulates the spirit of the beautiful game, offering a perfect setting for matches, practices, and the joy of playing football. Surrounded by lush greenery, it provides a serene and inspirational backdrop for both players and spectators. Sportpark Bijlmer at Mandela Park is where the love of football and the beauty of nature come together, creating memorable moments on and off the pitch.

Cruyff Court Ajax Joubertstraat

The Cruyff Court Ajax Joubertstraat in Bijlmer is not just a small football court; it's a gathering place for friends and a sanctuary for shared moments of joy and camaraderie. Here, amidst the fast-paced city life, you'll find a haven where friendships are forged and memories are made. It's where friends come together to kick the ball, cheer each other on, and relish the beautiful game that unites them. The court's ambiance, with its iconic Ajax legacy, inspires a sense of belonging and shared passion. As the sun sets behind the towering buildings, the Cruyff Court becomes the place where friendships deepen, laughter echoes, and bonds are strengthened through the universal language of football. It's a testament to how sport can bring people together and create lasting connections between friends in the heart of Bijlmer.
